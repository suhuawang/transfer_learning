9:00am Interesting Title of a Talk
Zhi Ming Lime (International Islamic College)
Zhi Ming Lim is a highly respected professor in the field of deep learning and network models. With a strong background in computer science and machine learning, he has dedicated his career to advancing the state of the art in artificial intelligence. Professor Lim's research focuses on developing novel algorithms and architectures for deep neural networks, which are at the core of many modern AI applications. He has published numerous papers in top-tier conferences and journals, and has received several awards and honors for his contributions to the field. In addition to his research, Professor Lim is also a passionate educator, and has taught courses on deep learning and related topics at both the undergraduate and graduate levels. He is known for his ability to communicate complex ideas in a clear and engaging manner, and is highly regarded by his students and colleagues alike.
9:25am Interesting Title of a Talk
Kian Wei Ng (Taylor's University College Malaysia)
Kian Wei Ng is a renowned expert in the field of deep learning and network models. He is a professor who has made significant contributions to the development of deep learning algorithms and architectures. His research has focused on the development of novel neural network models that can handle complex data structures and improve the accuracy of machine learning models. Ng has published numerous papers in top-tier conferences and journals, and his work has been cited extensively in the field of deep learning. He has also received several awards for his research, including the IEEE Signal Processing Society Best Paper Award and the NVIDIA Global Impact Award. As a professor, Ng is known for his innovative teaching style and his ability to convey complex concepts in a clear and concise manner. He is dedicated to training the next generation of deep learning experts and has mentored many successful graduate students and postdoctoral researchers. Overall, Kian Wei Ng is a highly respected figure in the field of deep learning and network models, and his contributions have greatly advanced the state of the art in these areas.
9:50am Interesting Title of a Talk
Heng Leong Tan (International Islamic College)
Heng Leong Tan is a prominent professor in the fields of deep learning and agriculture. He has extensive expertise in applying cutting-edge deep learning techniques to address complex agricultural challenges such as crop yield prediction, disease detection, and plant breeding. With over two decades of experience in research and academia, he has authored numerous research papers and book chapters in the fields of computer vision, machine learning, and precision agriculture. Dr. Tan is widely recognized as a leading expert in his field, and his work has been recognized with several prestigious awards and honors. Through his research, teaching, and mentorship, Dr. Tan is making significant contributions to the development of sustainable and innovative agricultural practices.
10:15am Interesting Title of a Talk
Jian Xiang Yap (International Islamic College)
Jian Xiang Yap is a professor who specializes in the fields of deep learning and biological engineering. He is a renowned expert in the use of artificial intelligence and machine learning to solve problems in the field of biological engineering. Dr. Yap has made significant contributions to the development of new computational tools and algorithms for analyzing complex biological systems. With a strong background in both computer science and biological engineering, Dr. Yap is uniquely positioned to bridge the gap between these two fields. He has published numerous papers in top-tier academic journals, and his research has been recognized with several awards and honors. Dr. Yap is a sought-after speaker and collaborator, and he has worked with researchers from a variety of fields to apply his expertise in deep learning to a wide range of problems. His research has the potential to revolutionize the way we understand and interact with biological systems, and he is widely regarded as one of the leading experts in the field of computational biology.
